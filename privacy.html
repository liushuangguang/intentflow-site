Privacy Policy for IntentFlow

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

IntentFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information in strict compliance with the Google Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies.

1. Information We Collect

To provide the core functionality of "Visual Task Flow & Focus Assistant," IntentFlow collects the following data:

A. Browsing Data (Essential for Functionality)

  • URLs and Page Titles: We access the URL and title of the webpages you visit.
    • Purpose: This metadata is sent to our AI engine to analyze your browsing intent and automatically categorize tabs into logical tasks (e.g., "Coding", "Shopping").
  • Technical Identifiers: We use local Tab IDs and Window IDs to manage the organization of your browser tabs. These are ephemeral and not linked to your identity.
  • Favicons: We access website icons to display them in the visual task tree.
  • Optional External Search Shortcuts: The extension provides Google/Bing/Baidu shortcut buttons in the side panel. A search URL is opened only when you click and submit a keyword.
    • Important: We do not change your browser's default search engine or new-tab settings.

B. Account Information (Optional)

  • Sign-In Data: If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address or phone number via our authentication provider (Authing/Supabase) to synchronize your task history across devices.
  • Guest Mode: You can use the extension entirely without signing in. In this mode, your task history is stored strictly locally on your device.

C. Usage Data (Analytics)

  • We collect anonymous usage metrics (e.g., feature usage frequency, error logs) to help us improve stability.
  • We use coarse location data (Region/Country) solely to provide localized pricing and language settings. We do NOT track your precise location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the Single Purpose of assisting your browsing workflow:

  1. Intent Analysis: To automatically group chaotic tabs into structured, visual task trees.
  2. Automation: To automatically open the side panel or organize tabs based on your settings (e.g., Tab Overload alerts).
  3. Search Assistance (Optional): To let you start external searches on Google/Bing/Baidu from the side panel when you explicitly click.
  4. Sync: To synchronize your curated task history across your devices (only if logged in).

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

We do NOT use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate IntentFlow. Your data is transmitted securely (HTTPS) to:

  • AI Engine (Cloudflare Workers & LLM Providers):
    • URLs and Titles are processed by our secure AI backend to generate classifications.
    • Privacy Guarantee: Data sent to the AI model is for inference only. We have configured our AI providers NOT to use your data for model training. Data is processed ephemerally and not permanently stored by the AI provider.
  • Supabase: Used for secure, encrypted database storage and user authentication.
  • Mixpanel: Used for anonymous product analytics.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Access Control: User data is protected by strict row-level security (RLS) policies in our database.
  • Local Storage: In Guest Mode, your browsing history remains on your device and is never sent to our database (only sent ephemerally to the AI engine for immediate classification).

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Pause Recording: You can toggle "AI Analysis" off at any time via the extension interface. When paused, no data is collected or sent.
  • Search Shortcut Control: If you do not use the Google/Bing/Baidu buttons, no shortcut search request is triggered by the extension.
  • Delete Data: You can delete specific tasks or your entire history directly within the extension.
  • Guest Mode: You can choose not to create an account to keep your history local.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the extension removes all local data from your browser.

6. Data Retention

We retain your synced task history only as long as you maintain an account with us. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed from our servers.
