Common Problems

  • Opening dozens of tabs and forgetting what you were doing.
  • Getting distracted and forgetting your original search intent.
  • Wanting to review your research path but can't remember the pages.

Solution

IntentFlow automatically records every page you visit and uses AI to analyze the intent, generating a clear Task Tree for you.

  2. Turn it on (Blue).

  1. Click "Load unpacked" (top left).
  2. Select the dist folder.
